About Me

I am a third year at UCLA double majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Philosophy, and I am a research assistant for the UCLA Computational Applied Math Department. I am advised by Deanna Needell.

My current research areas are martix/tensor factorization, topic modeling, computer vision, and deep learning.
